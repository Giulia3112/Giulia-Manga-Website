Identity

Four facets of the same restlessness

I

Founder

I build ventures to transform ideas into initiatives capable of impacting real systems. My founder journey began with Rearth, created during a global innovation competition, and evolved through Hult Prize and international ecosystems such as Silicon Valley.

Along the way, I realized that talent exists everywhere, but opportunity does not. From that inquietude, NATTA was born: a platform designed to democratize access to global opportunities.

ventures · impact · systems
II

Academic

Since early on, the pursuit of excellence has shaped my formation. During high school, I became a national medalist in Astronomy and Astronautics and served as a volunteer teacher in the field.

During a gap year dedicated to academic exploration, I earned a scholarship to the University of Cambridge, where I studied Physics. I began university in Aerospace Engineering before finding Business as my central intellectual field.

physics · cambridge · interdisciplinary
III

Inventor

I am driven by building products, systems, and technological solutions. At NATTA, I led the product journey from idea to MVP, participating directly in technical, product, and UX decisions.

This made me into a junior developer with an integrated vision of business and technology. I cultivate a strong interest in hardware and applied engineering, which also inspired long-term technical initiatives.

product · technology · engineering
IV

Artist

Writing has always been part of how I organize thought and express creativity. I produce nonfiction, have coauthored work connected to Operations Research and Game Theory, and have participated in literary competitions.

Alongside writing, I maintain a deeply personal drawing practice. My art works like a silent confession — for what cannot always be said directly.

writing · drawing · expression
